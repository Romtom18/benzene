The Benzene Value Chain

An interactive walkthrough from feedstock to final products.

Benzene Production

Process: Catalytic Reforming

$$\underset{\text{Hexane}}{C_6H_{14}} \xrightarrow[\text{Pressure, 490-530°C}]{\text{Pt/Re Catalyst}} \underset{\text{Benzene}}{C_6H_6} + 4H_2$$

↓

Benzene
($C_6H_6$)

Ethylbenzene

$$\underset{\text{Benzene}}{C_6H_6} + C_2H_4 \xrightarrow[\text{200-250°C}]{\text{Zeolite}} \underset{\text{Ethylbenzene}}{C_6H_5CH_2CH_3}$$

↓

Secondary Derivative: Styrene

Process: Dehydrogenation

$$\underset{\text{Ethylbenzene}}{C_6H_5CH_2CH_3} \xrightarrow[\text{620°C}]{\text{Fe}_2O_3} \underset{\text{Styrene}}{C_6H_5CH=CH_2} + H_2$$

Final Product Uses (from Styrene)
  • Polystyrene (PS): Packaging, insulation
  • SBR Rubber: Vehicle tires, footwear
  • ABS/SAN Resins: Lego bricks, electronics

Cumene

$$\underset{\text{Benzene}}{C_6H_6} + C_3H_6 \xrightarrow[\text{250°C}]{\text{Zeolite}} \underset{\text{Cumene}}{C_6H_5CH(CH_3)_2}$$

↓

Secondary Derivatives: Phenol & Acetone

Process: Hock Process

$$\underset{\text{Cumene}}{C_6H_5CH(CH_3)_2} + O_2 \xrightarrow{\text{Acid}} \underset{\text{Phenol}}{C_6H_5OH} + \underset{\text{Acetone}}{CH_3COCH_3}$$

Final Product Uses
  • From Phenol: Adhesives, polycarbonates
  • From Acetone: Solvents, acrylic glass

Cyclohexane

$$\underset{\text{Benzene}}{C_6H_6} + 3H_2 \xrightarrow[\text{150-250°C}]{\text{Ni Catalyst}} \underset{\text{Cyclohexane}}{C_6H_{12}}$$

↓

Secondary Derivative: Adipic Acid, etc.

Process: Multi-step Oxidation

Final Product Uses
  • Nylon 6,6 & Nylon 6: Carpets, textiles, fishing line, auto parts.

Nitrobenzene

$$\underset{\text{Benzene}}{C_6H_6} + HNO_3 \xrightarrow[\text{50-60°C}]{H_2SO_4} \underset{\text{Nitrobenzene}}{C_6H_5NO_2} + H_2O$$

↓

Secondary Derivative: Aniline

Process: Hydrogenation

$$\underset{\text{Nitrobenzene}}{C_6H_5NO_2} + 3H_2 \xrightarrow[\text{Catalyst}]{\Delta} \underset{\text{Aniline}}{C_6H_5NH_2} + 2H_2O$$

Final Product Uses (from Aniline)
  • MDI: Polyurethane foams
  • Chemicals: Dyes, pigments, rubber chemicals

Chlorobenzene

$$\underset{\text{Benzene}}{C_6H_6} + Cl_2 \xrightarrow{FeCl_3} \underset{\text{Chlorobenzene}}{C_6H_5Cl} + HCl$$

↓

Primary Uses

  • Solvent: For paints, adhesives, and chemical processes.
  • Intermediate: For dyes, herbicides, and pharmaceuticals.
  • Phenol Production: Via the Dow process (older method).
